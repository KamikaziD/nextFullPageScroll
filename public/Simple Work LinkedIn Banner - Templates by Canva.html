LinkedIn banners by Canva

With Canva, you can make your professional profile more engaging and memorable with one of our stunning LinkedIn banner templates. It only takes a few steps: choosing a ready-made LinkedIn banner image template, customizing the design as necessary, then downloading it for your LinkedIn page.

You can browse through our expertly-curated collection of cover photos for a LinkedIn header template that reflects your industry or your line of work. We have layouts suitable for various industries and professions. We also have sleek designs that have generic styles and themes but can be easily personalized to fit your needs and preferences. Our expertly-designed templates come in predefined sizes and are ready for publishing, so you don’t need design skills to create a banner that sets your profile apart.

With our drag-and-drop platform, you can customize any template to fit your needs. Edit the text to reflect your professional details, change the color scheme or choose a different background image. You can browse through our media library for images, shapes and icons. You can also opt to use your own photos by uploading them into your design. Need to collaborate with a colleague? Just send them a link and edit access to your design. After making your changes, you can save your new banner and upload to your LinkedIn account.
